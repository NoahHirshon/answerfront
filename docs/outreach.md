## Target Segment

Start with owner-led or founder-led service firms that depend on high-intent discovery:

- Managed IT service providers
- Cybersecurity consultancies
- Fractional CMO and B2B marketing advisory firms
- Local professional services with visible competitors

These buyers are likely to understand search, reputation, and comparison-shopping pain. They also have websites with enough public material to audit quickly.

## Qualification Rules

Prioritize prospects that have:

- A clear service category
- Named target geography or buyer type
- Competitor-rich search results
- Public proof points or claims that AI answers could miss
- Contact form or public business email

Avoid:

- Huge agencies with procurement-heavy buying
- Regulated firms where the audit may be mistaken for legal, medical, or financial advice
- Businesses with no visible website content to inspect
