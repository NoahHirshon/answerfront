# Answerfront Offer

## Positioning

Answerfront sells a practical AI search visibility audit for companies that need buyers to find, compare, and trust them in AI-generated answers.

The offer avoids broad SEO promises. It focuses on evidence:

- Which buyer-intent prompts mention the company
- Which prompts omit the company
- Whether AI answers describe the company accurately
- Which public pages, proof points, and structured content should be improved first

## First Customer Profile

Best-fit first customers:

- B2B services, agencies, consultants, niche SaaS, home services, legal-adjacent services, clinics, and local professional services
- Companies with an existing website and at least 2 competitors
- Companies that sell through comparison, reputation, specialization, or local visibility
- Owners, founders, marketing leads, and solo operators who can approve a `$149` purchase quickly

Avoid for the first batch:

- Regulated advice businesses where the audit might be mistaken for legal, medical, or financial guidance
- Ecommerce stores with huge catalogs
- Companies that need enterprise procurement
- Customers expecting guaranteed rankings or guaranteed AI citations

## Pricing

Initial price: `$149`

The price is low enough for impulse approval, but high enough to validate real willingness to pay. If the first 5 customers buy without sales friction, test `$249`.

## Customer Promise

Within 3 business days, the customer receives:

- A PDF snapshot of AI visibility across 10 buyer-intent prompts
- A competitor and citation gap summary
- A one-week action checklist
- A retest prompt set
